Cricket has specific rules governing gameplay, such as offside, fouls, and penalty kicks. Understanding these rules is essential for fair and competitive play.

Equipment and Gear
Cricket players typically wear cleats, shin guards, and jerseys during matches. The only required equipment is the ball, making it accessible to players of all ages.

Playing Field
