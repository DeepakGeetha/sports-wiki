Badminton Sports

Sport Overview
The Badminton is a game that can be played 1 to 1 or in a team 2/2.

Rules and Regulations
Badminton has specific rules governing gameplay, such as offside, fouls, and penalty kicks. Understanding these rules is essential for fair and competitive play.

Equipment and Gear
Badminton players typically wear cleats, shin guards, and jerseys during matches. The only required equipment is the ball, making it accessible to players of all ages.

Playing Field
Badminton is played on a rectangular field with goals at each end. The field is marked with lines for various purposes, such as the center circle and penalty area.

Famous Players
Badminton has produced many legendary players known for their skills and achievements in the sport. Some of the greatest Badminton players in history include Pele, Diego Maradona, Lionel Messi, and Cristiano Ronaldo.
