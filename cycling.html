Sport Overview
The Cycling is a game that can be played 1 to 1 or in a team 2/2.

Rules and Regulations
Cycling has specific rules governing gameplay, such as offside, fouls, and penalty kicks. Understanding these rules is essential for fair and competitive play.

Equipment and Gear
Cycling players typically wear cleats, shin guards, and jerseys during matches. The only required equipment is the ball, making it accessible to players of all ages.

Playing Field
Cycling is played on a rectangular field with goals at each end. The field is marked with lines for various purposes, such as the center circle and penalty area.
